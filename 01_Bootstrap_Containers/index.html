The Bootstrap Container(s)

Default Container

fixed-width container, means it's max-width changes at each breakpoint.

Responsive Containers -> specify a class that is 100% wide until the specified breakpoint is reached.

100% wide until the small breakpoint
100% wide until the medium breakpoint
100% wide until the large breakpoint
100% wide until the Extra large breakpoint
100% wide until the Extra extra large breakpoint

Fluid Containers -> full width container, spanning the entire width of the viewport
